VAT in UAE

At the beginning of 2018, VAT is to be introduced at the rate of 5% with some limited exceptions including basic food items, healthcare and education in the entire UAE. With the nascent nature of this legislation there are challenges involved in the implementation, proper documentation and the filing of returns under VAT. VAT is a business issue, which can impact all the areas of business from purchase to pay, cash to reporting.At Zenith, we will streamline your accounting and internal control systems in a professional manner with helping you prepare for the transformation ahead and making your organization VAT Profitable.
